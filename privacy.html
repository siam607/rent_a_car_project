Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your personal information

  1. Home
  2. Privacy Policy
Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when you use our car rental services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, physical address, date of birth.
  • Identification: Driver's license number, passport/NID number.
  • Payment Information: Credit/debit card details (processed securely via payment gateway), cash deposit records.
  • Rental Details: Pick-up/drop-off locations, dates, vehicle type, preferences.
  • Communication: Records of correspondence with our customer support team.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, cookies (see Cookie Policy).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Processing your car rental reservations and agreements.
  • Communicating with you about your bookings, account updates, and promotions.
  • Verifying your identity and driving eligibility.
  • Processing payments and managing billing.
  • Improving our services, website, and customer experience.
  • Complying with legal and regulatory requirements.
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

Under GDPR and similar data protection laws, we process your personal data based on:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our rental agreement.
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with tax, safety, and transportation regulations.
  • Consent: For marketing communications (you may opt out anytime).
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and prevent fraud.

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors, insurance companies, vehicle maintenance partners, and customer support platforms.
  • Legal Authorities: When required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

All third parties are required to protect your information with appropriate security measures.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information as long as necessary to:

  • Fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy.
  • Comply with legal obligations (typically 5-7 years for financial records).
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements.

You may request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention requirements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing.
  • Request a copy of your data (data portability).
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications.
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@rentcarbd.com.

7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission.
  • Secure payment processing through PCI-compliant gateways.
  • Access controls and authentication for internal systems.
  • Regular security audits and updates.

8. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status.
  • Analyze website traffic and user behavior.
  • Personalize content and advertisements.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we learn we have collected such data, we will delete it promptly.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries outside Bangladesh. We ensure adequate protection through standard contractual clauses and other safeguards.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. Please review their privacy policies before submitting personal information.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date will indicate the most recent revision. Continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
