About SSL Rent a Car BD

Your trusted partner for car rentals across Bangladesh

Our Story

Driven by Excellence

Founded in 2024, SSL Rent a Car BD began with a simple mission: to provide reliable, affordable, and safe car rental services to travelers across Bangladesh. What started as a small fleet of 5 vehicles has grown into one of the country's most trusted car rental companies.

Over the years, we've served thousands of satisfied customers - from tourists exploring the beauty of Cox's Bazar to business travelers navigating Dhaka's bustling streets. Our commitment to quality service, well-maintained vehicles, and transparent pricing has earned us a reputation as the go-to car rental service in Bangladesh.

Today, we operate in major cities including Dhaka, Chittagong, Sylhet, and Cox's Bazar, with plans to expand our reach to serve more communities across the country.
